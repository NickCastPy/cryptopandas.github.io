Crypto Pandas

Crypto Pandas is a collection of 10,000 code-based generated NFTs created by a fourteen-year-old programmer Nick. Each Panda is a unique digital collectible with a specific combination swinging on Matic Mainnet.

Our Purpose

There are less than 2000 pandas which are living in the wild. The purpose of this project is to save the Pandas from disappearing from our world and to assure them a wonderful & safe life in the wild 🎋
